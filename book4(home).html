This is a 26 track double album pressed on blue/purple splatter vinyl, limited to 400 copies.

Released on Milan Records - multi platinum selling British artist/ producer Labrinth scores the first season of the hit HBO series. Written and recorded in close collaboration with the show’s writer Sam Levinson, his original compositions feature prominently throughout the series. The collection is a genre blending mix of gospel, soul and electronic influences, indicative both of Labrinth’s inimitable style as well as the show’s storyline. The album features 5 vocal tracks. 'Euphoria' follows up Labrinth’s debut album 'Electronic Earth' and his collaborative album with LSD.
